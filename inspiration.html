Why This Inspires Me

This clip always inspires me because it connects deeply to my experience serving a two-year LDS mission in Uganda. While “Hakuna Matata” might sound like a simple, playful phrase, its Swahili meaning is "no problem."

I met people who embraced a “no problem” attitude despite facing real poverty, uncertainty, and hardship. Their resilience taught me that Joy isn’t the absence of problems but the ability to carry hope through them. That’s why the message of Hakuna Matata resonates so much with me.
